I used a codepen I found to mix two colours that were dominant in the shoot for Rezareez.

It generates new "pieces" everytime you reload it or press the 'erase' button. You can also choose to export the current image. It will go as wide as your screen allows it to go.

The original codepen wasn't responsive, I made the canvas responsive. It also didn't have most of the randomized features I added. I wanted to make sure you wouldn't see the same "piece".
